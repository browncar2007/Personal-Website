About Me

My name is Caronn Brown, and I use he/him pronouns. I am currently a first-year Allen Scholar at the University of Washington, pursuing a degree in computer science. My passion for this field comes from my uncle, who owns a computer repair business. My goal is to earn my degree and eventually take over his business, continuing his legacy while also bringing in my own knowledge and ideas to help it grow. This connection between family, technology, and my future has motivated me to work diligently and remain committed to my studies.

Outside of academics, I have a wide range of hobbies that keep me balanced and motivated. I played varsity baseball and varsity golf in high school, which taught me the importance of teamwork, discipline, and perseverance. I also enjoy chess, where I recently reached a 1000 Elo rating in rapid, reflecting my problem-solving mindset. In my free time, I enjoy playing video games, especially Street Fighter 6, where I primarily play as Akuma. Music is also an essential part of my life—I was a snare drum player in my schools drumline, which gave me a strong sense of rhythm and collaboration. All of these interests shape who I am and help me bring both creativity and dedication to everything I do.
